I adore Brazil's beaches

But the beaches in the Northeast Brazil have a special place in my heart

It's impossible to talk about Brazil and don't think about its beaches and great weather.
The country has incredible places for beach lovers.
The Brazilian Northeast has a coastline that stretches over 1,800 miles, featuring secluded beaches with calm and warm waters and palm trees. Another great thing about the Northeast is that the sun shines most days in this region and can be visited year-round.
